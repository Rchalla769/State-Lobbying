Available Lobbying Charts
Registered Lobbyists by Year and Branch : Shows the total number of individuals registered to lobby in Louisiana.
Reported Lobbying Expenditures by Month and Branch Lobbied : Shows the total amount of lobbying expenditures reported by registered Louisiana lobbyists. The chart shows totals by month for a calendar year and gives the option of which year and branch(es) to display.
Reported Subject Matters Lobbied by Month and Branch : Shows the total number of registered lobbyists reporting to have lobbied about a particular subject matter in a given month. The chart gives options to select the month and branch to display.
